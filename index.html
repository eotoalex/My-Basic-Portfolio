About Me

My journey through web development began in high school, when my father got me a Dell D600 laptop. Ever since then I have been studying computer programming and hardware throughout my undergraduate study of English Literature and my Masters in Secondary English Education. The web development courses provided by Columbia University exposed me to the frameworks and best practices used in modern computer programming, enabling me to apply my Javascript background to newer technologies. I am always learning something new every day. I hope you are as well.
